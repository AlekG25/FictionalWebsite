Team Members

CEO: Countess Katarzyna Zamoyski

Countess Katarzyna Zamoyski, a descendant of noble Slavic lineage, brings regal elegance and unwavering determination to ChronoSlavic Expeditions. With a passion for both history and humor, Countess Katarzyna envisions a world where every traveler can experience the charm of Slavic history with a touch of royal flair.

CFO: Duchess Yelizaveta Kovalenko

Duchess Yelizaveta Kovalenko, known for her charm and grace, takes on the crucial role of CFO. Her financial acumen ensures the smooth financial operations of ChronoSlavic Expeditions, securing the resources needed for each enchanting expedition.

COO: Baron Ivan Petrovich

Baron Ivan Petrovich, a stalwart supporter of Russian heritage, holds the position of COO. His responsibilities include overseeing the day-to-day operations, ensuring that each tour is meticulously planned and executed to perfection.

Chairman of the Board: Prince Mieszko Radziwiłł

Prince Mieszko Radziwiłł, a modern-day aristocrat with a keen interest in Slavic heritage, serves as the guiding force behind ChronoSlavic Expeditions. His vision is to intertwine the richness of Slavic history with the joy of time-travel, creating an experience that is both educational and entertaining.

Royal Director: Princess Ekaterina Romanova

Princess Ekaterina Romanova, a descendant of the illustrious Russian Romanov dynasty, graces ChronoSlavic Expeditions with her royal presence. As the Royal Director, she oversees the overall vision and ensures that each tour is a regal journey through time.

Noble Strategist: Prince Dmitry Volkov

Prince Dmitry Volkov, a charismatic leader with Ukrainian roots, serves as the esteemed Noble Strategist. His role involves shaping the strategic direction of the company, reflecting his passion for preserving Ukrainian cultural heritage.

Chief Security Guardian: Count Viktor Drakonov

Count Viktor Drakonov, an esteemed noble with a background in security, assumes the role of Chief Security Guardian. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to ensuring the safety of every traveler, Count Drakonov safeguards the noble expeditions through time.
